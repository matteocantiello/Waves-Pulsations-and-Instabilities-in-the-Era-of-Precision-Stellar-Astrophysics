Rotation angular velocity plotted as a function of fractional radius \(x=r/R\) , for a \(4.21\,M_{\odot}\) model starting from a non-rotating state and subject to 1,000 years of angular momentum transport by a dipole ( $\ell=1$) g mode (blue curve). The red curve shows the profile of a stellar model set uniformly rotating at the ZAMS, with an angular velocity tuned to reproduce the surface angular velocity of the blue curve.
