Left: Theoretical SPB Instability strip calculated with MESA and GYRE. The growth rate \(\eta\) indicates the strength of the opacity driven instability for dipolar (\(\ell=1\)) modes. Top Right: Propagation diagram for a MESA stellar model in the middle of the SPB instability strip.  The horizontal black line indicates the frequency \(\nu_g\)of a typical SPB g-mode, which can propagate below the Brunt-Vasailaa and the Lamb frequency (Blue line) . Bottom Right: the black solid line shows the critical magnetic field strength needed to strongly alter dipolar g-modes of frequency \(\nu_g\). The green dashed line shows the predicted magnetic field in the radiative region outside the convective core calculated assuming equipartition fields generated via an MS dynamo.
